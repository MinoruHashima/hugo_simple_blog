country: "Croatia"
source: "The Dubrovnik Times"
summary: "On January 30th, museums across Croatia will open their doors for a special evening event. This tradition, which began in 2005, marks its 21st edition this year. Over 250 venues will participate, offering free admission from 6 PM to 1 AM. In Dubrovnik, 15 locations are taking part, including public art galleries and museums, historic fortresses and palaces in the Old Town, and the privately-run Love Stories Museum. Late-night buses from Pile will also be increased for the occasion."
